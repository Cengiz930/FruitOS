ğŸ›¡ How to Disable and Enable Antivirus Temporarily

Windows Defender

1. Open Windows Security.

2. Go to Virus & threat protection.

3. Click Manage settings under Virus & threat protection settings.

4. Turn off Real-time protection to temporarily disable.

5. Turn it back on when done to protect your PC.

Avast

1. Right-click the Avast icon in the system tray.

2. Choose Avast shields control.

3. Select duration to disable shields temporarily.

4. Re-enable shields the same way after installation.

AVG

1. Right-click the AVG icon in the taskbar.

2. Click Disable protection.

3. Enable protection after installing the software.

Norton

1. Open Norton Security dashboard.

2. Navigate to Settings.

3. Turn off Auto-Protect temporarily.

4. Turn it back on after installation.

McAfee

1. Open McAfee console.

2. Go to Real-time Scanning.

3. Turn off scanning temporarily.

4. Enable scanning back after setup.

Bitdefender

1. Open Bitdefender dashboard.

2. Click Protection > Shield.

3. Turn off shield temporarily.

4. Turn on shield after installation completes.
