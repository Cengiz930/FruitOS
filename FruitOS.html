How to Temporarily Disable and Re-enable Common Antivirus Programs

Windows Defender (Microsoft Defender)

  • Open Windows Security from the Start menu.
  • Go to Virus & threat protection ‚Üí Manage settings.
  • Turn off Real-time protection.
  • It will turn back on automatically or after a restart.

Avast Antivirus

  • Right-click the Avast icon in the system tray.
  • Select Avast shields control.
  • Choose to disable for 10 min, 1 hour, or until restart.
  • Re-enable by selecting Enable all shields.

AVG Antivirus

  • Right-click the AVG icon.
  • Click Temporarily disable protection.
  • Select duration and confirm.
  • Re-enable from AVG dashboard.

Norton Antivirus

  • Open Norton ‚Üí Go to Settings.
  • Disable Auto-Protect temporarily.
  • Turn it back on later from the same menu.

McAfee Antivirus

  • Open McAfee Security Center.
  • Go to Real-Time Scanning ‚Üí Turn Off.
  • Select time, confirm. Enable again manually.

Bitdefender

  • Open Bitdefender dashboard.
  • Go to Protection panel.
  • Disable Shield or Autopilot temporarily.
  • Turn it back on via the same panel.
